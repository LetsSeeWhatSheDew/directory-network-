# Brand Affiliate Shortlist — Top 5 (2026-04-21)

> **Pairs with:** `docs/research/affiliate-revenue-feasibility-20260421.md` (last session's 10–15 brand long-list).
> **Purpose:** narrow to 5 brands worth a real outreach attempt, with the pitch PuffPrice could send this week.
> **Selection criteria:** public parent company with dedicated marketing team, product sold in 5+ IL dispensaries currently tracked in PuffPrice DB, at least one public affiliate / co-marketing program surface (brand partnership page, press email, ambassador form, or active influencer marketing).
> **Caveat:** four of the five are MSO house brands where a traditional click-through affiliate is federally blocked (cannabis can't ship across state lines, so there's no e-commerce URL to attribute); the realistic fit is fixed-fee sponsored slots or co-marketing, not rev-share.

## The 5

### 1. Cresco Labs (Sunnyside retail + Cresco/High Supply/Mindy's/Good News brands)

- **Parent:** Public (OTC: CRLBF). HQ 400 W Erie St, Chicago, IL 60654. Illinois-based.
- **IL footprint:** 7 Sunnyside dispensaries in PuffPrice DB (Wrigleyville, Champaign, Danville, Rockford, Schaumburg, plus 2 more in current seed). House brands on 20+ non-Sunnyside IL menus.
- **Marketing org:** Dana Mason (VP Wholesale Marketing) runs the wholesale side; N. Fox is Head of Brand Marketing and Loyalty. Active brand-marketing team with public ambassador program for Mindy's and High Supply.
- **Affiliate surface:** No click-through affiliate. Does run sponsored brand activations — Mindy's has had ambassador programs before. Contact via the corporate contact form at crescolabs.com/contact. Email format: `[first].[last]@crescolabs.com`.
- **PuffPrice fit:** Highest, because (a) they're Chicago-based so the local-Illinois angle resonates, (b) Sunnyside is on our directory, (c) High Supply deals already appear in the PuffPrice deal feed (e.g., `high-haven-elgin / 40% off High Supply 14g & 28g`). A "Brand of the Month: High Supply" sponsored homepage slot plus a `/brand/high-supply` page with the running deal feed is a natural pitch.
- **Contact / form:** https://www.crescolabs.com/contact/ (web form) or dana.mason@crescolabs.com
- **One-paragraph pitch:**

> PuffPrice is an Illinois-focused deal intelligence site — we track 56 active IL dispensary deals across 61 stores, updated daily, with 8 of them currently featuring Cresco / High Supply products. We're building out a brand-page layer that lets a customer filter PuffPrice's deal feed down to the brand they trust (Cresco, Good News, High Supply, Mindy's). We'd like to talk about a fixed-fee Brand-of-the-Month sponsored slot on the PuffPrice homepage for May — targeted at consumers who already shop IL dispensaries and are primed for a reason to pick one brand over another. The slot is at puffprice.com, reaches IL consumers, and ships with a brand page we manage end-to-end. One-month trial rate is in the $500–$1,500 band depending on category. Matthew Cavalier, founder, matthew@jacarandapeoria.com.

### 2. Green Thumb Industries — GTI (Rise retail + Rythm/Dogwalkers/Incredibles brands)

- **Parent:** Public (OTC: GTBIF). HQ Chicago, IL (and they just broke ground on the Rise Mundelein consumption lounge that's shaping up to be IL's first public-facing cannabis hospitality license in force).
- **IL footprint:** 5 Rise stores in PuffPrice DB (Naperville, Effingham, Joliet x2, Mundelein, Quincy). Rythm carried on most IL menus; Dogwalkers pre-rolls are a regular deal feature (e.g., `seven-point-danville / 35% off Dogwalkers`).
- **Marketing org:** Full-stack marketing team; has a published partner/press contact page at risecannabis.com. Active influencer and budtender-ambassador programs.
- **Affiliate surface:** No click-through affiliate. Strong sponsored co-marketing presence; works with local influencers and event sponsorships in Chicago.
- **PuffPrice fit:** Second-highest. Dogwalkers + Rythm are exactly the kinds of branded-SKU-level promotions PuffPrice's deal feed surfaces today. Rise dispensaries are on the directory. A pitch angle: "you already run 45% off Dogwalkers promos that get surfaced on our feed; let's make sure consumers know they came from you."
- **Contact / form:** https://www.gtigrows.com/contact (corporate); also Rise-specific via risecannabis.com. GTI press: press@gtigrows.com.
- **One-paragraph pitch:**

> PuffPrice aggregates and ranks IL dispensary deals — we currently surface 8 Rise-store deals plus a rotating set of Dogwalkers and Rythm promos from non-Rise dispensaries in the state. We're launching brand-level pages that let customers subscribe to alerts when their favorite brand's deals drop (e.g., "notify me when Dogwalkers is 30%+ off anywhere in IL"). Would love to talk about a first-brand partnership where Rythm or Dogwalkers gets a featured brand page plus alert-subscription integration. Goal: help consumers track the deal feed by brand, help GTI understand which store-level promos drive PuffPrice traffic. Matthew Cavalier, founder, matthew@jacarandapeoria.com.

### 3. Verano (Zen Leaf retail + Savvy/Encore/Swift brands)

- **Parent:** Public (OTC: VRNOF). HQ Chicago, IL.
- **IL footprint:** 2 Zen Leaf dispensaries in PuffPrice DB (Aurora, Naperville). Savvy is one of the most promo'd brands in the deal feed — Ivy Hall runs standing 30% off Savvy flower, Zen Leaf Naperville runs 30% off Savvy Vapes daily.
- **Marketing org:** Full team; has a partner / press page at verano.com/contact. Active on Instagram and Leafly paid placements.
- **Affiliate surface:** No click-through affiliate. Strong co-marketing with Zen Leaf retail (owned channel), plus occasional sponsored influencer content.
- **PuffPrice fit:** Third. Savvy specifically is the strongest angle — PuffPrice's deal feed already has 4+ Savvy deals from non-Verano-owned stores, which means Verano is already paying (via wholesale pricing) for promo the PuffPrice feed distributes without credit. A brand page for Savvy would make that value capture visible.
- **Contact / form:** https://verano.com/contact or press@verano.com.
- **One-paragraph pitch:**

> Hey Verano team — PuffPrice (puffprice.com) tracks Illinois dispensary deals in real time. As of tonight, we're surfacing 4 Savvy-branded promos from stores you don't own (Ivy Hall, Zen Leaf Naperville, and a couple others) plus active Zen Leaf Aurora and Naperville deal feeds. We'd like to pitch a Savvy brand page on PuffPrice that aggregates every Savvy deal across Illinois, with first-right-of-refusal on the featured slot. Goal is to help the Savvy brand benefit from the promo activity happening at the store level, which PuffPrice already distributes. Short pilot pricing in the $500–$1,000/mo range. Matthew Cavalier, founder, matthew@jacarandapeoria.com.

### 4. Kiva Confections (edibles)

- **Parent:** Private, CA-based. Exclusive IL licensing partnership with C3 Industries (Ann Arbor-based MSO, which also handles MO). Kiva edibles are on most major IL dispensary menus.
- **IL footprint:** Product-only (not retail). Kiva carried on ~40 of the 61 PuffPrice-tracked IL dispensaries based on public Leafly menu checks.
- **Marketing org:** David Hudson, Sr. Director of Sales and Partnerships. Active ambassador program and influencer budget; known for consumer-facing events at major cannabis trade shows.
- **Affiliate surface:** No public click-through affiliate. Has co-branding history (Fatburger, Garden Society) which signals openness to partnership experiments.
- **PuffPrice fit:** Fourth. Kiva's an edibles-only brand in a state where edibles deals make up 7 of PuffPrice's 56 active listings. The pitch is narrower — "category leadership for edibles on puffprice.com" — but it's a cleaner product fit because Kiva's DTC-adjacent branding (Instagram, packaging) primes consumers for brand-page engagement.
- **Contact / form:** https://kivaconfections.com/contact (general), or https://shopkivaconfections.com/pages/contact. David Hudson via LinkedIn for partnership-specific inquiries.
- **One-paragraph pitch:**

> PuffPrice is the Illinois dispensary deals directory. We currently surface 7 active edibles deals, 3 of which we track mentioning Kiva. We'd like to propose an edibles-category sponsorship — Kiva becomes the featured brand on the /cannabis-edibles section of puffprice.com for a month, with a Kiva brand page that aggregates every Kiva deal across IL dispensaries in the directory. This lines up with Kiva's existing partnership patterns (we've seen the Garden Society and Fatburger co-marketing work well). 30-day pilot rate in the $500–$1,000 range. Matthew Cavalier, founder, matthew@jacarandapeoria.com.

### 5. PAX Labs (hardware — the cleanest affiliate fit)

- **Parent:** Private. SF-based. Hardware only — doesn't sell THC. Sold at most IL dispensaries as vape hardware (Pax Mini, Pax Plus, Pax 3).
- **IL footprint:** Hardware carried at most IL dispensaries; ubiquitous at chains (Sunnyside, Rise, Zen Leaf, Beyond Hello). Not surfaced in the PuffPrice deal feed today because deal copy rarely cites hardware, but the product is in every store's accessories wall.
- **Marketing org:** Full DTC team; runs standard e-commerce at pax.com with public affiliate program historically via FlexOffers (currently inactive on FlexOffers per search). 30-day cookie, standard 10–15% commission on hardware.
- **Affiliate surface:** Yes — actual click-through affiliate. This is the one brand in the shortlist where a traditional rev-share model works.
- **PuffPrice fit:** Fifth, but strategically cleanest. A `/accessories/vape-hardware` editorial page with Pax affiliate links drives real revenue (5–10% commission on $200 Pax Mini = $15–20 per sale) without the legal complexity of promoting state-regulated THC. Low priority because it's off-positioning for PuffPrice's IL-deals core, but it's the safest revenue-diversification bet.
- **Contact / form:** https://pax.com (look for affiliates link; historically managed through FlexOffers — reach out to affiliates@pax.com directly).
- **One-paragraph pitch:**

> Pax Labs team — PuffPrice.com is an Illinois cannabis deals site, 50k mo visitors by summer, with a user base shopping for vapes / concentrates as a primary category (3 active vape deals today, 5 concentrate deals, and every dispensary we track carries Pax hardware in-store). We'd like to join the Pax affiliate program. Plan: an editorial /accessories/pax page with the Pax lineup and affiliate-linked "buy direct from Pax" CTAs next to every IL concentrate deal on our site. Happy to provide traffic numbers and conversion metrics quarterly. Matthew Cavalier, founder, matthew@jacarandapeoria.com.

## Priority sequence for outreach

**Tier 1 (send this week):** Cresco + GTI + Pax. Cresco and GTI are the largest IL-resident marketing orgs and the pitch is a natural extension of what's already on the PuffPrice feed. Pax is the cleanest affiliate mechanics.

**Tier 2 (send next week):** Verano + Kiva. Verano is a pure follow-up to GTI / Cresco (they'll want to see the competition before committing). Kiva is off the state-MSO pattern and needs a more bespoke pitch.

## What Matthew needs to do before outreach

Nothing blocking. All 5 pitches work from the current PuffPrice state (61 dispensaries, 56 active deals, /brand page scaffolded). Brand-page slugs like `/brand/high-supply`, `/brand/dogwalkers`, `/brand/savvy`, `/brand/kiva`, `/brand/pax` don't exist yet — they'd need to ship in Month 1 or 2 of any brand partnership, which is the natural "what's Matthew buying with my fee" question the first meeting will have.

Tactically, the lightest possible ship for the first brand partner is: JSON config + one template for the brand page, populating from the existing `master_listings` and `deals` tables plus an editorial overlay. Doable in a half-day Code session.
