## Long description (drop-in for `master_listings.long_description`)

High Haven Elgin, operating under the storefront name "The Record Store," is a recreational cannabis dispensary at 15 Clock Tower Plaza in Elgin, about 45 minutes northwest of downtown Chicago via I-90. The "Record Store" concept is the visible piece — the store leans into a music-themed interior and the High Haven brand has given each of their IL locations a similar named persona (The Puff Palace in Normal, for example). Underneath the theme, this is a standard adult-use dispensary selling flower, pre-rolls, vape carts, concentrates, edibles, tinctures, and topicals from Illinois-licensed brands.

Elgin pricing is noticeably friendlier than Chicago proper because Kane County applies a lower local cannabis tax rate than Cook County plus Chicago municipal. Customers drive in from Schaumburg, South Elgin, St. Charles, Bartlett, Streamwood, and the nearby Randall Road retail corridor specifically for this reason. The store is near the Grand Victoria Casino on the Fox River and within the Clock Tower Plaza retail block, which gives easy parking and predictable drive-in access from Route 31 and I-90.

Hours run 9 AM to 9 PM Sunday through Thursday and 9 AM to 10 PM on Fridays and Saturdays — common IL dispensary hours, with the weekend late-close being the meaningful one. An on-site ATM is available; debit card transactions process as cashless-ATM withdrawals, which is standard for Illinois cannabis retail. The store runs a loyalty program called the High Rollers Club (20% off for first-time customers who join) alongside the standard Illinois discount categories: medical cardholders, veterans, students, and birthday.

Two deals are currently live: 45% off select flower this week and 40% off High Supply 14g and 28g flower. The High Supply promotion is brand-specific — High Supply is the value-tier flower line from Green Thumb Industries (GTI), and 14g/28g quantities make this a meaningful per-gram price drop for bulk buyers. Between the two deals, flower is the category to prioritize on this trip.

## FAQs (for `/l/high-haven-elgin` detail page)

### Does High Haven Elgin accept debit cards?
Yes, via cashless-ATM transactions (a standard workaround for Illinois dispensaries since federal law blocks most card networks from processing cannabis payments directly). Expect a small ATM fee (usually $2–$4). An on-site ATM is available if you'd rather pull cash and pay that way.

### What's parking like?
Clock Tower Plaza is a standard suburban retail strip with a large shared lot. Parking is free and plentiful — this is not a city-center constraint. Accessible parking is at the front of the plaza near the entrance.

### Is High Haven Elgin recreational, medical, or both?
Both. Adults 21+ with a valid government-issued photo ID can purchase recreational product. Illinois medical cardholders get state excise tax breaks and often faster menu access on limited-allocation drops.

### What is the "High Rollers Club" and is it worth joining?
It's High Haven's loyalty program. First-time customers get 20% off when they enroll at the register. Ongoing members accumulate points toward future discounts. If you're making a single recreational visit it's worth the minute it takes to sign up for the first-visit discount alone.

### How much is the 40% off High Supply flower deal actually worth?
High Supply 14g packs typically retail around $120–$140 in Illinois, and 28g (ounce) packs around $200–$260. A 40% discount puts those in roughly the $72–$84 (half-oz) and $120–$156 (oz) range, depending on the specific strain and the store's starting price that week. Always check the per-gram price at the register to compare apples-to-apples.
