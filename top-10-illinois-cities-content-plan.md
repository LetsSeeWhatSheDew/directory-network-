# Project Green — Top 10 Illinois Cities by Cannabis Search Potential

**Date:** April 9, 2026
**Goal:** Prioritize city pages for maximum organic traffic capture

---

## Ranking Methodology

Ranked by a composite of: estimated search volume for "dispensary in [city]" and related queries, number of licensed dispensaries (supply = demand signal), city population, and strategic factors (border traffic, university towns, highway crossroads).

Illinois has 271+ licensed dispensaries across 66+ cities as of March 2026. Statewide sales hit ~$1.5B in 2025.

## Top 10 Cities

### 1. Chicago
- **Population:** ~2.7M (metro ~9.5M)
- **Dispensaries:** 20–30+ (most in state)
- **Why #1:** Highest absolute search volume by far. Queries like "dispensary near me chicago," "chicago dispensary menu," "weed delivery chicago" dominate Illinois cannabis search. Multiple neighborhoods each worth their own page eventually (Lakeview, River North, South Loop, Wicker Park).
- **Key Competitors in SERPs:** Leafly, Weedmaps, Yelp, do312.com
- **Target Keywords:**
  - "chicago dispensary" (primary)
  - "dispensary near me chicago"
  - "best dispensary in chicago"
  - "chicago recreational dispensary"
  - "weed dispensary chicago IL"

### 2. Rockford
- **Population:** ~148K
- **Dispensaries:** ~7
- **Why #2:** Third-largest city in IL. Borders Wisconsin, where recreational cannabis is still illegal — draws significant cross-border search traffic from southern WI (Beloit, Janesville, Madison). "Rockford dispensary" captures out-of-state demand.
- **Target Keywords:**
  - "rockford il dispensary"
  - "dispensary near me rockford"
  - "recreational dispensary rockford illinois"
  - "dispensary near wisconsin border"

### 3. Springfield
- **Population:** ~114K
- **Dispensaries:** ~5–15
- **Why #3:** State capital, central IL hub. Draws from a wide rural catchment area. Government workers, tourists visiting state landmarks, and regional residents all search.
- **Target Keywords:**
  - "springfield il dispensary"
  - "dispensary in springfield illinois"
  - "best dispensary springfield il"
  - "cannabis dispensary near me springfield"

### 4. Peoria

  - "recreational weed peoria"

### 5. Naperville

## Content Page Outline (Template for Each City)

Each city page should follow this structure to maximize SEO value:

### Page Title Format
`Dispensaries in [City], Illinois — Your Complete Guide (2026)`

### URL Structure
`/illinois/[city-slug]` (e.g., `/illinois/chicago`, `/illinois/rockford`)

### Section Outline

**1. Hero / Intro (100–150 words)**
- H1: "Dispensaries in [City], Illinois"
- Brief overview: how many dispensaries, recreational vs. medical, what to expect
- Quick-reference box: total dispensary count, avg price range, hours

**2. Dispensary Directory Listings (dynamic from Supabase)**
- Listing cards pulled from database
- Each card: name, address, hours, phone, rating, "recreational/medical" badge
- Sort: featured → pro → free tier
- CTA: "Claim this listing" for unclaimed dispensaries

**3. Cannabis Laws in [City] (200–300 words)**
- Local ordinances (some IL cities have additional restrictions)
- Purchase limits (recreational: 30g flower, 5g concentrate, 500mg edibles for IL residents; half for out-of-state)
- Consumption rules specific to the city/county
- ID requirements
- Internal link to a future `/illinois/cannabis-laws` hub page

**4. What to Expect at a [City] Dispensary (200–300 words)**
- First-time visitor guide
- Typical product categories: flower, edibles, concentrates, topicals, pre-rolls
- Average price ranges for the area
- Payment methods (cash, debit, ATM on-site)

**5. Neighborhoods & Nearby Areas (150–200 words)**
- For larger cities: which neighborhoods have dispensaries
- Nearby cities with dispensaries (internal links to their pages)
- Driving distances from neighboring states if applicable

**6. FAQ Section (4–6 questions, schema markup)**
- "How many dispensaries are in [City], IL?"
- "Can out-of-state visitors buy cannabis in [City]?"
- "What are dispensary hours in [City]?"
- "Do I need a medical card to buy cannabis in [City]?"
- "What's the cheapest dispensary in [City]?"
- "Can I order online for pickup in [City]?"

**7. SEO Footer Content (100–150 words)**
- City-specific context: why [City] is notable in the IL cannabis scene
- Long-tail keyword integration
- Internal links to related city pages

### Technical SEO Requirements
- Schema markup: LocalBusiness + FAQPage
- Meta title: "Dispensaries in [City], IL | Find Cannabis Near You (2026)"
- Meta description: 155 chars, include city name + "dispensary" + differentiator
- Open Graph image: city-themed (skyline, map pin, etc.)
- Canonical URL set properly
- Internal linking: each city page links to 3–5 neighboring city pages

### Content Specs Per Page
- **Total word count:** 1,000–1,500 words of static content
- **Dynamic content:** Dispensary listings from Supabase
- **Images:** 1 hero image (city), 1 map embed, listing photos from DB
- **Estimated production time:** 2–3 hours per page (research + write + build)
